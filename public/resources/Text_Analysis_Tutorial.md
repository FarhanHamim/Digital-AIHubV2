## Common Analysis Techniques

### Word Frequency Analysis
Count how often words appear in your text to identify important topics.

### Sentiment Analysis
Classify text as positive, negative, or neutral to understand public opinion.

### Topic Modeling
Discover hidden themes in large collections of documents.

### Named Entity Recognition
Extract names of people, places, organizations, and other entities.
